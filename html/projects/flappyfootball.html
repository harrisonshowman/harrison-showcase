The Idea

Started as a weekend project to learn Flutter, combining my love for football with the addictive mechanics of flappy-style games. The goal was to create something simple yet engaging that could showcase Flutter's capabilities.

Technical Challenges

Implementing smooth physics, optimizing for different screen sizes, and creating a responsive control system that feels natural on both iOS and Android devices. The Flame game engine proved perfect for handling sprites and collisions.

Launch & Growth

What started as a learning project became a hit in the app stores. Regular updates with new features, seasonal themes, and community feedback integration helped grow the player base organically.
