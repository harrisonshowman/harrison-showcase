WineAbout is a modern mobile app for wine journaling and discovery. Users can track their wine experiences, rate and review bottles, and explore new wines through a clean, intuitive interface. The app is designed for both casual drinkers and wine enthusiasts looking to document and share their wine journey.

Development Process & My Contributions

I served as the front-end and client-side developer for WineAbout. My responsibilities included:

  • Building the mobile UI with React Native, focusing on a seamless and visually appealing user experience.
  • Implementing navigation, state management, and integration with Firebase for real-time data sync.
  • Developing custom components for wine journaling, search, and social features.
  • Collaborating with designers and backend developers to ensure a cohesive product vision.
  • Testing and refining the app based on user feedback and usability studies.
