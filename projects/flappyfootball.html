Flappy Football is a mobile game inspired by the classic Flappy Bird, but reimagined with a fun football theme. Navigate the ball through obstacles and aim for your high score in this addictive arcade experience, built with Flutter and Dart.

Features

  • Simple one-touch controls
  • Colorful football-inspired graphics
  • Dynamic obstacles and increasing difficulty
  • High score tracking
  • Quick restarts for nonstop fun
